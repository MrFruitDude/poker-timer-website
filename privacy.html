Privacy first

Your tournaments stay on your devices.

PokerTimer was architected so no personal data leaves your screen unless you explicitly share it.

Last updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

We built PokerTimer to be privacy-safe by default. The app operates locally and does not transmit data to our servers.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics or cookies
  • Payment details inside the app

3. Local Storage

Tournament structures, player counts, and preferences are stored on your device and can be deleted at any time from the settings panel.

4. Optional Sync

If you enable iCloud or OneDrive sync, your data is encrypted and managed by the platform provider. PokerTimer never receives a copy.

5. Children’s Privacy

PokerTimer is intended for users 13+ and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Updates

We may revise this policy to reflect new features. Changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.

7. Contact

Questions? Reach out and we’ll respond swiftly.
